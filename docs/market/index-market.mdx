Palm Studio's API enable the creation and management of primary and secondary NFT markets aka Dropsites.

* Primary market: marketplace where issuers lists their NFTs or collections and where an investor acquires them - Source.
* Secondary market: marketplace where investors can sell NFTs they purchased on a primary market or bought NFTs from other investors.
